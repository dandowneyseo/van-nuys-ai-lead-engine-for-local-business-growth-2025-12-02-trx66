Do I need to pay for ads or marketing to use this system?

No! That's one of the biggest advantages of our system. The AI Lead Engine generates leads organically without requiring paid advertising spend. You only pay a single monthly subscription fee, and the system does all the work. This is completely different from Google Ads or Facebook Ads where you pay per click or impression. How does the AI know which leads are qualified for my business?

Our AI system is trained using your specific business criteria, industry data, and past customer profiles. During setup, you tell us exactly who your ideal customer is, what they're looking for, and what qualifies as a good lead. The AI then uses advanced machine learning to identify prospects matching these criteria across your local market. As the system processes more leads and gets feedback on which ones convert, it continuously learns and improves its qualification accuracy. This means your lead quality gets better over time.

Can I customize the follow-up sequences and messaging?

Absolutely! Customization is one of our core features. You can personalize all follow-up messages, adjust timing between messages, set specific criteria for different follow-up paths, and even create multiple sequences for different types of leads. Our intuitive dashboard makes it easy to make changes without any technical knowledge. You can A/B test different messages to see what resonates best with your audience, and the system will automatically optimize based on response rates.

What if I'm not satisfied with the results? Is there a guarantee?
